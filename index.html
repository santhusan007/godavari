About Gokarna

Located in the southwestern Indian state of Karnataka, Gokarna is a popular pilgrimage destination for Hindu known for its pristine beaches and breathtaking landscapes. Gokarna welcomes hordes of tourists from around the world in search of sanctity and relaxation.







About Us

Located at the heart of Gokarna, THE GOVADRI HOTEL delights its guests with classic and blissful experiences. We welcome our guests with warm hospitality and attractively equipped rooms. A wide range of 32 rooms that includes Deluxe, Luxury, Suite & Dorematery to our Chef- driven Pure Veg. Restaurant 'NAVRATNA', a spacious Banquet Hall with a Bar, Lawn area, Swimming Pool with a kids section, a Yoga & Spa center, etc, is what make THE GODAVARI a centerpiece of Gokarna.
